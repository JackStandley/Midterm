Achievements:

At only 19 years old Standley has accumulated numerous milestones throughout his short life. Here are a two examples:

MVP Season

In his freshman season at Fort Zumwalt West baseball season, Jack earned the Most Valuable Player Award. This was due to his numerous shutdown performances on the mound, and his .330 batting average at the plate. Nothing could've stop Jack that year as he lead his team to a second place finish in the GAC champtionship.

Art Awards

In his younger years Jack was quite the painter. When he was only 11 years old he was honored at the St. Louis Art Museam for his 'Picture the Music' piece called "Big Music". he then came back next year and did it again with a spin off of his last piece. Jack has recently resparked his love for art as he was head of art for the homecoming float.
